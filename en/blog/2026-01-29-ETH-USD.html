What Drives Ethereum? Beyond Daily Swings

Despite the current dip, Ethereum's long-term value proposition remains robust, rooted in strong fundamentals. As the world's leading smart contract platform, Ethereum is the backbone of decentralized finance (DeFi), non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and a vast ecosystem of decentralized applications (DApps). Ongoing network upgrades, such as the Dencun upgrade, continue to enhance its efficiency and scalability, providing a strong foundation for future growth.

Furthermore, the persistent discussions surrounding the potential approval of an Ethereum spot ETF could open doors for mainstream institutional adoption, bringing substantial new capital into the market. These structural factors underpin Ethereum's long-term trajectory, irrespective of daily price fluctuations.
