About WiseAIWiseU

Last updated: March 6, 2026

🎯 Our Mission

WiseAIWiseU is an independent dividend investment research platform dedicated to helping individual investors make informed, data-driven decisions about US dividend stocks and long-term passive income strategies.

We believe that access to quality financial data and educational content should not be limited to institutional investors. Our goal is to bridge that gap — providing retail investors with the analytical tools and knowledge that were previously available only to professionals.

🛠️ Technical Foundation & Data Methodology

WiseAIWiseU is built on a foundation of data engineering and IT systems expertise. Our platform aggregates and processes financial data from verified, industry-standard sources including:

  • Yahoo Finance API — Real-time and historical stock price data, dividend history, and financial ratios
  • SEC EDGAR — Regulatory filings for earnings and payout verification
  • Public financial databases — Cross-referenced dividend records for accuracy

Our proprietary S/A/B/C grading system scores dividend stocks based on a composite algorithm that evaluates: Dividend Yield sustainability, Payout Ratio, consecutive years of dividend growth, Free Cash Flow coverage, sector outlook, and stability score. All grades are algorithmically generated and reviewed periodically.

📚 Educational Focus

Beyond stock screening, WiseAIWiseU is committed to financial education. Our platform includes:

  • Snowball Calculator — Simulate compound interest and DRIP growth over time
  • Dividend Calendar — Track upcoming dividend payment dates
  • Market Insights Blog — Daily analysis of high-interest stocks and market trends
  • FAQ & Educational Guides — Beginner-friendly explanations of key dividend investing concepts

All educational content is produced with the goal of explaining complex financial concepts in accessible, beginner-friendly language.

🌐 Global Reach

WiseAIWiseU serves investors across the world with full multilingual support in English, Korean (한국어), and Portuguese (Português). All content — including stock analysis, educational guides, and legal pages — is fully localized for each language.

We understand that international investors face unique challenges, including foreign withholding taxes, currency risk, and access to US markets. Our platform addresses these topics directly with region-specific educational content.
