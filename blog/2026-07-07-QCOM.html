Risk Factors

Despite its glowing potential, several risk factors should be closely monitored by potential investors:

  • Customer Concentration Risk: Qualcomm still derives a substantial portion of its licensing and chip revenue from a few key premium smartphone makers, such as Apple, which is actively working on developing its own in-house modems.
  • Geopolitical and Supply Chain Vulnerabilities: With major manufacturing partners based in Taiwan and a significant share of revenue coming from the Chinese market, any escalation in geopolitical tensions could severely disrupt Qualcomm's operations.
  • Intense Competition in AI and Automotive: Competitors like MediaTek in mobile, and Nvidia and AMD in the PC and automotive space, are aggressively vying for dominance, which could pressure Qualcomm's margins over the coming years.
